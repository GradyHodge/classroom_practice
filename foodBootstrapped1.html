Top 5 Favorite Restaurants

Best Local Places to take your friends from out of town

Tejas de Brazil

Looking for an upscale experience to wow your friends who have a voracious apetite and you need something more than all you can eat wings? If money is no object, go you can find one of the finest dining/buffet experiences at TEJAS DE BRAZIL

Menu Reviews

Jin Beh

If you are searching for a great local sushi restaurant where the sushi chefs can prepare works of edible art, then sit yourself down in front of the sushi bar and order off the menu. You'll be surprised at what personal creativity they come up with to wow you at Jin Beh.

Menu Reviews

Rock Fish

Forget Red Lobster, if you are looking for the real dea, someone who knows how to cook fish, any fish, the right way,(which is hard to come by in TX) then find the nearest ROCK FISH.

Menu Reviews

Hutchins BBQ

Got friends form out of town and you want to give them a taste of Texas real BBQ. Then hunker down at the multi-award winning Hutchin's BBQ for $25 all you can eat smoked bbq.

Menu Reviews

Chuy's TexMex Cantina

One of THE most special cultural foods found in Texas is the mingling of anglicized authentic Mexican dishes, better known as Tex-Mex. For a place with culture, (and easy on the pesos), you'll have a blast at Chuy's TexMex Cantina.
